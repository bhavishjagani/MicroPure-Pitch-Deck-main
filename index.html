02 · The Problem

You drink a credit card
of plastic every week.

Microplastics are now in our oceans, soil, food, blood, and lungs. They're under 5 mm — invisible to the eye and almost impossible to count without expensive lab equipment. Detection is the bottleneck. You can't fix what you can't measure.

5g
Plastic ingested per person per week (Senathirajah et al., 2021)
24T
Microplastic particles already in the ocean (NOAA / 2023)
80%
Of tested human blood samples contain microplastics (Leslie 2022)
Why nobody is solving it
  • Lab equipment costs $100k+ — out of reach for schools, NGOs and most utilities.
  • Manual microscopy takes hours per sample and needs a trained technician.
  • Cloud AI tools want your data — privacy and bandwidth are real blockers.
  • Raw counts mean nothing to a parent, teacher or city council.
  • No standard risk language exists for "is this water actually safe?"
03 · The Solution

Point a microscope. Drop the image. Get a verdict.

MicroPure AI is a browser-based dashboard backed by a YOLOv8 detector trained on microplastic imagery. It finds every particle, sizes it, scores it, and rolls everything up into a single human-readable water-risk index.

Detect
YOLOv8 finds particles down to a few pixels and classifies size (nano → large).
Quantify
Per-particle confidence, area, and density per megapixel — not just a count.
Score
4-factor risk index (count, confidence, density, size) capped so one box ≠ 100%.
Visualize
Bounding-box overlay, gauge, charts, per-particle log. Export JSON / CSV / PDF.
Local-first
Runs on your laptop. Images never leave your machine. No subscription required.
Open API
Single /predict endpoint — drop into any pipeline or LIMS.
